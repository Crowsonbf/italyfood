Breakfast Panini

  1. 2 eggs, cooked over easy
  2. 2 tablespoons Mezzetta Napa Valley Bistro Homemade Style Basil Pesto
  3. Â½ cup Mezzetta Roasted Red Peppers, drained and patted dry
  4. 2/3 cup arugula

Directions

Preheat panini press. If adding pancetta to your sandwich, place pancetta slices in panini press and cook about 3 minutes or until crispy. Remove from press and set aside. To assemble the paninis, add a tablespoon of pesto on the top inside slice of each sandwich. Add the eggs to the bottom slice of bread. Top the egg with arugula, pancetta, and a layer of roasted red pepper. Top the sandwich with the pesto bread slice and place into the panini press for about 5 minutes or until slightly crispy and golden.
