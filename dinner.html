Chicken Tortilla Soup

  1. 1 jar (6.5 oz.) Mezzetta Hot Chili Pepper Everything Spread
  2. 1 qt. chicken stock
  3. Â½ rotisserie chicken, pulled and shredded
  4. Salt and pepper, to taste
  5. Optional Toppings: Crunchy tortilla strips, diced avocado, shredded cheese, sour cream, diced onion, cilantro

Directions

In a medium soup pan, combine an entire jar of Everything Spread with the chicken stock and shredded chicken. Mix thoroughly over medium high heat and bring soup to a simmer. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve hot with your favorite toppings.
